ABOUT US

Welcome to DAWAT, your destination for authentic Indian Dishes. Since 2010, we’ve been sharing our love for hearty, homemade meals inspired by the vibrant flavors of Tuscany. Our menu features signature dishes like Hydrabadi Biryani and Davangere Dose, crafted with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. Whether you’re here for a casual lunch, romantic dinner, or family gathering, our warm ambiance and attentive service ensure a dining experience you’ll cherish. Come savor the taste of India with us at DAWAT!
